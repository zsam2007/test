A word from our Founder and Chairman. 
Mr. Mostafa Sokar

إهتمامنا بنجاح صناعة الغزل و النسيج يأتي من حرصنا الدائم علي تقديم افضل المنتجات لشعب مصر الكريم حيث اننا نفخر بتقديم افضل ما تجود به الطبيعة من خامات بعد تصنيعها بأحدث التقنيات العلمية و التكنولوجية لنكون دوماً في المقدمة. كذلك رغبتنا الحثيثة في دفع عجلة التنمية الإقتصادية   لبلدنا الحبيب كانت و ستظل دوماً أسمى أهدافنا    

Our Interest in the success of the textile industry comes from being always keen to present the best prodcuts to our beloved people in Egypt, as we are proud to present the best of what nature can offer us after fabricating it with the best ways scientifically and technologically to always be in the lead. Also our sincere desire to push forward our counties’ economic development was and will always be our highest target.

HISTORY THAT WE ARE PROUD OF 

The start of Sokar Tex 

Sokar Tex is a family owned business that was dreamt of by Mr. Mostafa Sokar back in the 90s. This dream was meant to be realized by the early years of the 21st century. 

from start to being a leader 

with his family and responsible sons around him Mr. Mostafa taken off and flew over the competition and became the leader in the Egyptian market of textile industry
